Holiday Movie and Shows Tier List

Rupsa Biswas

November 23rd, 2025

Thanksgiving

1. Charlie Brown Thanksgiving – Charlie Brown originally planned to have Thanksgiving dinner with his grandparents, but his plan is put to a stop when Peppermint Patty invites herself, Marcie, and Franklin over to his house, assuming he is hosting for the holiday. He, Snoopy, and Woodstock work together to create an unusual last-minute Thanksgiving feast.

2. Free Birds - Jack, a presidentially pardoned turkey, and Reggie, another turkey, discover a time machine and set out to remove turkeys from Thanksgiving dinner all while creating laughs in the audience.

3. Garfield’s Thanksgiving – Garfield can’t wait to stuff his stomach for Thanksgiving, but things take an unexpected turn when Jon brings back Garfield’s new strict vet home for the holiday dinner. Can Garfield still celebrate Thanksgiving with a feast, and can the dinner succeed even without Jon’s terrible cooking skills?

4. It’s the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown – Linus fully believes in the ‘Great Pumpkin’, but when he finds out no one else believes in it, he sets out to prove its existence by staking the night out alone in a pumpkin patch.

5. A Fuller Thanksgiving (Season 2, Episode 6) – DJ wants to host the perfect Thanksgiving dinner, but things get hard with the whole family, including Joey and Ginger’s rowdy children. Can Thanksgiving still be saved with all the chaos?

Christmas

Elf - Buddy, who never fit in at the North Pole, sets off to find his true family after finding out he’s actually human. The thing is, Buddy’s never been in human society before, and is kind of a misfit.

Home Alone Universe - Any of the movies in the franchise are a hilarious watch as Kevin (or Alex or Max) outmaneuvers burglars (either criminal or simple mistaken citizens) to save Christmas–a true Christmas classic.

Polar Express - A young boy boards a mysterious train and encounters unexpected challenges and meets new friends.

How the Grinch Stole Christmas (animated and live action version) - Another Christmas classic , as the Grinch learns the warmth and spirit of Christmas.

A Christmas Carol (Scrooge) - Ebenezer Scrooge is visited by the three ghosts of Christmas–the past, the present, and the future–to show him the spirit of Christmas. If Scrooge doesn’t change, however, he will die a lonely, despised death.

Disney's The Santa Clause (movie and tv show) - When Scott accidentally kills Santa and puts on his robe, he becomes Santa. He needs to convince his family and the world that he’s not crazy as he begins to transform into Santa himself. The rest of the movies are a continuation of the first one about his life and challenges as the new Santa. The show series is about his late wife and his journey to find a successor.

Noelle - Santa’s daughter sets out on a journey to explore and attempt to find her brother, the to-be-next Santa after Santa himself dies.

Beethoven's Christmas Adventure - Beethoven, an adorable dog, sets out on a funny adventure to retrieve the magic bag of toys from two crooks who stole it to save Christmas.

Casper's Haunted Christmas - Casper, ordered by the ghost king, must scare at least 1 person before Christmas. His uncles, knowing his friendly personality, send his look-alike cousin, Spooky, but things don’t go as planned…

Klaus - Jesper, after becoming the worst student at the academy, is exiled to a remote town, where he meets a recluse toymaker named Klaus.
