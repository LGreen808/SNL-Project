Top 5 Gifts for Teenagers in 2021

Elizabeth Whitman

December 20, 2021

The holiday season has come around again and everyone is scrambling to find the perfect gift for their loved ones. Teenagers can be especially difficult to shop for. So, these are some ideas of what is popular for them.

First, there is the Kodak Luma Projector. It can turn any blank wall into a big screen television. This is the perfect gift for pretty much anyone who likes watching TV or movies. It is a little pricey at about $220, but can provide hours of entertainment.

The next gift is the Beats Studio Buds. These are wireless noise cancelling earbuds that are compatible with Apple and Android phones. These would be great for listening to music and watching videos. The earbuds, which are $120, are also a little expensive, but would be great for music lovers.

Another great gift is the Wemore Shaggy Weighted Blanket. Recently, weighted blankets have become very popular. They are perfect for cold winter nights. This blanket is $70 on Amazon, but similar ones can be found online to fit any budget.

Next, is the Fujifilm Instax Mini Camera. Cameras that take Polaroid style pictures have been popular for a very long time and continue to be popular. Unlike a phone camera, this camera also prints out the picture that is taken. This way, the photo can be kept and put into a frame or album. This camera can be found at Target for $70, but similar cameras can be found online.

Finally, a good gift is a board game called Gloomhaven: Jaws of the Lion. This is a game of strategy that can be played with up to four players. It would be a lot of fun to play with friends and family. It can be found on Amazon for about $30.

These are some gift ideas for teenagers that fit a variety of budgets. All of these gifts can be purchased online, which makes holiday shopping much more convenient. Teenagers are sure to love these gifts. Happy shopping and have a great holiday season!
