About

Welcome to the website of Marlboro High School Newspaper, The Hitching Post and the Marlboro High School Literary Magazine. We run out of Marlboro High School through a club called The Hitching Post. The Hitching Post is for news, sports, student life, etc and the Literary Magazine is for artwork, poems, short stories, etc. We make a new edition every month and at the end of each school year we create a Senior Edition dedicated to the Senior Class of that year. Any student in Marlboro High School can join the club! And write stories, articles, poems, and even make artwork for the Hitching Post and The Literary Magazine!.
